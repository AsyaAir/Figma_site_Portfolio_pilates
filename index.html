What actually I love to do

Privet classes

If you are looking to accelerate your health and well-being, the 1:1 classes are the fastest way to get results. With individual sessions, we can exercise at your level and progress the difficulty as you improve. Private sessions also allow you to develop a rapport with me so I will know when you are having a good day and be pushed a bit further and gain an understanding of what motivates you helping you to gain the best results.

Group classes

Private Pilates session with me for three people. The session is guided by the group's wellness goals. The session includes a mix of reformer, tower, barrel equipment, and/or small props.

Public classes

Mixed Level Pilates classes in Trumbull Physical Therapy and Welness Ceter. We are a synergistic group of independent practitioners whose mission is to provide individualized healing in a supportive environment.
